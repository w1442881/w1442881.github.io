Navigating with split-screen browsers

This site was designed to take advantage of split-screen functionality in modern browsers. Rather than complicate the layout of the site, it is very easy to take advantage of split-screen features for browsing reference content in particular. The instructions for Microsoft Edge are provided here, but are similar to other browsers.

  1. Find the main menu of your browser, usually an ellipsis on the top navigation bar: ...
  2. Select Split screen
  3. Browse as you normally would (usually on the left screen). If, while browsing content, you find a reference or linked content you'd like to inspect without leaving your place on the web page, right-click and select Open link in right split screen
